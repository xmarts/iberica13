Product Cost Price History

This module useful to show the history of the cost price for the product, you can also track the history of the cost price of the product for different suppliers. Easy to find rates given to you by the supplier in the past for that product.

Features

Easy to specify no of items you want to keep as the previous history.

Easy to filter records by the status of the purchase order (confirmed, done or both).

Useful to get costs price given by suppliers for the product with supplier, qty, etc important details.

Version

v13.0.1 (22th October 2019)
  • Initial Release

Inside purchase settings set Item limit given as the number of record display inside product "Purchase Price History".On based on select "Order Confirm" to show record only from confirm purchase order, select "Done (Locked)" to show record only from the locked purchase order, if both options selected shows record only from confirm purchase order and locked purchase order."

Inside Products in new tab "Purchase Price History" purchase order history of that product available as per item limit and record selection given.

Inside Product Variant in new tab "Purchase Price History" show only record of that variant only.
